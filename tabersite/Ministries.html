VBS

We have Sunday School in English and Odia with three different grades. Experienced teachers are taking classes and giving training in songs, skits, etc. During summer holidays, we conduct VBS in selected towns and villages for the moral and spiritual upliftment of the children. Resources persons trained in children's ministry are giving leadership to this VBS Program. 

LITERATURE MINISTRY

We involve in distributing and promoting Christian literature in vernacular language through translating books to local language and putting stalls in different book fairs and exhibitions.

YOUTH GROUPS

Youths are the cream and backbone of the society. Majority of our church members are students and youngsters. Through our Youth Ministry, we encourage and guide youngsters to share their concerns and help them to go more closer to God. Ocassionally, we conduct Youth Seminars and Retreat for their spiritual upliftment and wholistic development.

EVANGELSIM

We conduct study classes, camps and retreats for the genuine seekers. It helps them for their spiritual growth and maturity.

FAMILY ENRICHMENT

In the present context, family disputes, divorces, broken families are very common. As solution to this problem, we conduct family seminars and workshops to build healthy families on the basis of biblical principles.

RELIEF WORKS

We are involved in relief works during the time of natural disasters and calamities.

CHRISTMAS EVENTS

Christmas meetings are conducted at different places during December every year.
